Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer free consultations with our design experts. You can schedule an appointment by calling us or filling out the contact form above. Our experts will help you choose the right tiles for your space and provide professional advice on design and installation.

Can I request tile samples before making a purchase?

Absolutely! We understand that seeing and feeling the tiles in your own space is important. We offer sample services for most of our tile collections. There may be a small fee for samples, which is refundable upon purchase. You can request samples in our showroom or through our contact form.

Do you provide installation services?

While we don't directly employ installation teams, we have a network of trusted professional installers that we can recommend. These installers are experienced with our products and maintain our high standards of quality. We're happy to connect you with them for your installation needs.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unused tiles in their original packaging within 30 days of purchase. Custom orders and clearance items are non-returnable. A 15% restocking fee may apply. Please keep your receipt for all returns and exchanges. For more details, please contact our customer service team.

How do I maintain and clean my tiles?

Maintenance varies depending on the type of tile. Generally, regular cleaning with a pH-neutral cleaner is recommended for most tiles. Avoid acidic or abrasive cleaners that can damage the tile surface or grout. We provide specific care instructions with each purchase, and our team is always available to answer any maintenance questions you may have.
