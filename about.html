About Us

Our Story

The journey of Paldima Resources Limited from a small family business to a leading tiles showroom

From Humble Beginnings

Paldima Resources Limited was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality tiles with exceptional customer service. What began as a small shop with just a handful of tile options has grown into one of the region's premier tile showrooms.

Growth and Innovation

Over the years, we've expanded our collection to include thousands of tile options from around the world. We've pioneered innovative display techniques that allow customers to better visualize how tiles will look in their spaces, and we've built relationships with the finest tile manufacturers globally.

Today's Vision

Today, Paldima Resources Limited continues to maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that has been our hallmark since day one. We combine traditional values of craftsmanship and service with modern design trends and technology to offer an unparalleled tile shopping experience.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Paldima Resources Limited

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our products. Every tile in our showroom meets our strict standards for durability, design, and craftsmanship.

Integrity

We believe in honest business practices, transparent pricing, and keeping our promises. Our reputation is built on trust and integrity.

Innovation

We continuously seek out new designs, materials, and technologies to offer our customers the latest in tile trends and solutions.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to exceed expectations and create lasting relationships through exceptional service.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible practices and partner with manufacturers who share our dedication to sustainability.

Community

We believe in giving back to the communities we serve through charitable initiatives, local partnerships, and community engagement.
